Lesson Overview

Foundations of Algebra: Introduction to Variables and Basic Expressions for JSS 1 students.

inventory_2

MATERIALS NEEDED

  • Interactive Whiteboard / Chalkboard
  • Physical counters (beads or stones)
  • Worksheets: "The Language of Math"

Procedure

1. Introduction
schedule 10 MINS
Start with a real-world scenario: "If I have a bag of hidden mangoes and add 3 more, how do I describe the total?" Introduce the concept of a variable (x) to represent the unknown.
CORE
2. Core Activity
schedule 25 MINS
Collaborative Exercise: Students work in pairs to translate English phrases into algebraic expressions (e.g., "five more than a number" becomes n + 5). Use counters to visualize.
3. Conclusion
schedule 05 MINS
Quick wrap-up: Exit ticket where each student must write one original algebraic expression based on their daily routine.
