Lesson Preview: Algebraic Expressions

Lesson Overview

Summary

This lesson introduces students to the basic concepts of algebraic variables, constants, and terms, laying the foundation for equation solving.

Vocabulary

Variable Coefficient Constant Expression

inventory_2 Materials Needed

  • Whiteboard & markers
  • Algebra tile sets (1 per group)
  • Worksheets for practice
  • Digital projector for visual aids
Step-by-Step Procedure
1
Introduction & Hook
10 Mins

Start with a real-world scenario (e.g., buying items at a market where prices aren't fixed). Introduce variables as "placeholders" for unknown values.

2
Core Activity: Expression Building
20 Mins

In small groups, students use algebra tiles to represent verbal statements (e.g., "three more than a number") as algebraic expressions. Teacher circulates to check understanding.

3
Conclusion & Exit Ticket
10 Mins

Summary of key terms. Students write one algebraic expression on a post-it note to represent "five times a number minus two" before leaving.
